Wolf started UberWriter as part of the Ubuntu App Showdown a long time ago. Then a Master degree in Robotics at ETH Zurich got in the way of fulltime development on UberWriter. Now he works full time on interactive computing and open source HPC libraries at QuantStack in Paris: check out xtensor, xsimd and xframe! ;)

Manuel started colaborating in UberWriter after it's development was stopped a couple years ago, finishing its migration to the last libraries and contributing to package it with Flatpak. Currently he is a student finishing a degree in Physics at the University of Valencia, doing research into the holographic microscopy field.
